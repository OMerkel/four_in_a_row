Rules

Connect 4 is a two-player alignment game played on a vertical 7x6 board.

Goal

Be the first to create a line of four pieces of your color.

Lines can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

Turn Sequence
  1. Choose one of the seven columns.
  2. Your piece falls to the lowest empty slot in that column.
  3. Players alternate turns until someone has four in a row or the board is full.
Draws and End of Game

If all slots are filled and no line of four exists, the game is a draw.

When a game ends, open the menu and choose New Game to start again.

Options

Changes take effect immediately on AI turns and on new games.
